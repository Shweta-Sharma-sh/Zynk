Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

At Infynite, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, username, email, password.
  • Activity Data: Posts, comments, likes, shared content.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device info, IP address.
  • Optional Info: Profile details, interests, profile picture.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your Infynite account.
  • To improve user experience and personalize content.
  • To provide safe and secure access to features.
  • To monitor violations and improve platform safety.

4. Sharing Your Information

Infynite does not sell your data. We only share information when required for:

  • Legal purposes or government requests.
  • Platform security and fraud prevention.
  • Improving site performance using trusted services.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance performance, improve navigation, and personalize your experience. You can disable cookies through browser settings.

6. Data Protection & Security

Infynite uses secure encryption and monitoring systems to protect user data from unauthorized access. However, users must keep their passwords confidential.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Update or edit account information
  • Disable or delete your account at any time

8. Changes to This Policy

Infynite may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the platform means you accept the changes.

For any questions or privacy concerns, email us at privacy@infynite.com.
